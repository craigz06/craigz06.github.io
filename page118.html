First Camping in Roadtrek Chase
October 29, 2021

We camped on a double lot (45) at Lake Powhatan Campground. Our first night in the RV was great, the propane heater worked well, but the sleeping bag zipper broke and we made plans to mod the bathroom to make it larger. The next night we came home and were surprised to find a water snake in out bedroom right next to Rose. I quickly killed and removed it. Yikes!
