Idea Amplification

These seedlings all have the potential to grow into our world as real objects and processes. There are defined stages of a typical idea's life as it evolves from thought to reality. Here we will see the analogous  features of radio signal and idea amplification. An idea may be stored in biological or technological states that allow it to be accessed. They take many forms yet may be for analysis reduced to the ordered steps of an algorithem  with the understanding that this porcess is to be applied to some facet of world problems. One aspect of this concept is its memetic inherit traits that cause it to reprlicate. The rules of Dawkin's Darwin apply, for the environment is a limited and competitive one, where poor ideas are often killed by the delete key envoking a small magnetic field of erasure.
