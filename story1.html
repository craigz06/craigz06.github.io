Home Run! 240 Ferguson Cove Loop

location: Clyde, NC

date: September 28, 2022

An Opportunity Unfolds: The moment the property was listed, Liz's intuition kicked in. She knew that good land in the Crabtree farmland community was like a rare gem, quickly discovered and eagerly claimed. Without hesitation, she and Craig jumped into their car and drove west on I-40 from Asheville to get a glimpse of the prospective dream. Anticipation danced in the air as they ventured closer to their potential haven, and the excitement of the unknown propelled them forward.

A Serene Arrival: As they approached the property, the gravel driveway guided them alongside a babbling brook. The soothing sound of flowing water serenaded their journey, setting the tone for the tranquility that lay ahead. It was a mesmerizing welcome, and as they continued down that path, the whispers of the stream spoke of peace and possibilities. In that moment, they knew they were falling in love.

A Glimpse of History: Amidst the trees, a weathered yet sturdy barn emerged, a sentinel of the past overlooking a delightful pasture. It stood there, a testament to the stories and memories held within its timeworn walls. The barn whispered tales of hard work, harvests, and generations gone by. It was a sight that spoke of the property's character and the opportunity to breathe new life into its rich history.

Capturing the Beauty: While Craig aimed his camera to capture the sweeping views from the barn, the landscape unfolded before him like a masterpiece. The rolling hills, the lush greenery, and the endless sky created a symphony of sights that spoke of boundless potential. With every click of his camera, he preserved a moment of profound beauty, forever etching it into their hearts.

A Steep Obstacle As Craig followed Liz's direction, he couldn't help but feel a sense of apprehension. Could he possibly build a driveway up this formidable hill without running into layers of impenetrable rock? Nevertheless, determination propelled him forward, and he embarked on the ascent, step by step.

An Unforgettable Discovery When he reached the hill's summit, he found Liz gazing out at a breathtaking, unspoiled view that overlooked the sprawling farmland of the Pigeon River Valley. It was a sight that took their breath away.
