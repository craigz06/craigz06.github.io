Gloria Claus Stone

My mother, Gloria Claus Stone, peacefully passed away March 28, 2021 at the age of 97. She was born September 13, 1923 in Buffalo, NY. She spent her childhood in New York and Ridgeway, Ontario, Canada, then moved to Atlanta after marrying Dr. Steven G. Cline in 1946. Later she married Dr. William D. Stone in 1967 and they enjoyed many wonderful years together in Atlanta and at Lake Allatoona. She was an accomplished artist, organic gardener, world traveler and a great cook.
