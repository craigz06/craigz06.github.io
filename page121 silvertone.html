Speakers:

PYLE PLG6C 6.5" 400W 2 Way Component Speakers Set Power System 
6.5-Inch Mid-Bass Speakers
These mid-bass speakers pump out 400 watts of power, and with standard 6.5-inch sizing. A brilliant yellow poly injection cone provides great rigidity and resistance to harsh weather conditions. A non-fatiguing butyl rubber surround and high-temperature aluminum voice coil mean you'll get years of performance without the wear and tear. The speakers are housed in electroplated steel baskets, and boast a 40-ounce magnet.

1/2-Inch Tweeters
The included 100-watt 0.5-inch tweeters have three mounting options and a frequency response from 1.3 to 22 kHz. The neodymium magnet and silk dome construction help enable clear, well-defined high-end. Choose from flush, surface, or angle mounting to optimize your vehicle's mounting locations and get the best possible treble focus.

Passive Crossover Networks
Also included are a pair of passive crossover networks, with a 12 dB octave slope, to separate the signal from your head unit between the mid-bass and tweeter speakers for a richer, more accurate sound.
