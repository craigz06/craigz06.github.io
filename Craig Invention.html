First 3D Image from Moon

While working for Nimslo 3D Camera company, NASA sent me pictures of Apollo 15 mission. From the 2-1/4" slides I made 3D pictures of Scott and Irwin saluting on the moon. These are prob. the first 3D photograph from the moon made by accident, as one astronaut held still while the other snapped multiple pictures. The other image was created as the spacecraft moved. Yes, it feels like you are really there!
